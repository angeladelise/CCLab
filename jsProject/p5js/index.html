Ever wonder about what people post around the world?
Now you can see it for yourself. Wander is a new application where you can see what photos people post on Instagram from anywhere in the world!

This application was originally designed to analyze the differences in cultural behaviors through the digital medium. Do you think individuals from Beijing would post more food photos than individuals from Paris? Do you think people from Iceland would post more photographs of landscapes than people from Peru? Whether you question these behaviors or are just curious, Wander is the application to use!

Now go ahead and Wander around the globe!
